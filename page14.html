1. Is it easy to get the information you need in the organization?
2. Do you wish you could more easily analyze your project results?
3. Do you have a written list of all the reports that you use on a monthly basis, along with the criteria and the audience?
4. Do you struggle to produce the reports you need?


    ACEDI's highly experienced team can help you with both the technical aspects of information management and the integration of information and communication technology with the overall programmatic process. We come to your organization and help your staff learn what they need to know to accomplish their work more efficiently and effectively, while at the same time building their skills and the overall integrity of the data management system.

ACEDI can conduct a Knowledge Management Assessment to examine your use of technology in communicating internally and externally on programme issues and also on such key issues as reporting capabilities, data integrity, staffing and skills, workflow analysis, integration of systems, and documentation. ACEDI has proven track record that you can depend on to assist you in determining the most useful standard reports, creating the queries for those reports and establishing up a system for regular reporting to internal staff, Board, and other audiences.
